Why vinyl records?

Vinyl makes you step back in time

Vinyl is a unique and subjectively the best way to listen to music. Espically music recorded in mono. All recordings were recorded in mono until stereo was introcuded as an innovative recording technology in 1958. To listen to a mono record of music orgiginally released in mono gives you the opportunity to hear how everone heard this music for the first time. Any record has a particular richness to the sound that a CD or streaming service simply can not replicate. It gives the music texture, warmth, and depth that is one of a kind.
